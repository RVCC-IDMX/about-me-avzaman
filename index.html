In this house there sits a dog. That dog sits in such a manner. A mannerly manner. A manner in which I have no likes for, no desire for, no yearning for. If anything at all my dreams align with those of a dog not to be sittingin there. There in that chair. That chair sitting there much like a dog. That dog. A dog is the preventative of the accomplishments of a billionair and in exchange for that a companion. Yes I will sit next to this dog.

Through the brush there stands a tree. A standing tree made to stand and made for standing. As it stands for hapenstance that silly tree indeed is in need of me. For I provide all of what is needed for a tree indeed. INDEED. Indeed indeed I do and I will do for a long, oh so very long, time. Stamping away like elephants stamping away towards other elephants. Unruly is the folk that refuses to stamp and clamp and trot and cant. For those whomst cannont stamp cannont also, in turn, clamp, chant, cant, or rant. Perhaps it is best this way, that these who cannont do, not for it will affect those who cannot of the utmost rather than those whom they intend to harm. They cannont harm for they cannnont champ. Much like I. Though I refuse. Nobody thinks I am in such a position. Weary am I from all of this. Bones hollow. Flgihted. Bored. Unknowingly the best at what I do and yet unkown to be the best at what has been done. It is a tree that stands and I next to this tree. Forever shall I stand and forever it will be.
